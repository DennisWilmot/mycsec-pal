## Design Context

### Users
CSEC learners practising timed papers and reviewing marked work. They need confidence before submission and clear, actionable teaching after marking.

### Brand Personality
Warm, credible, intelligent, and encouraging. MyCSECPal should feel like a thoughtful Caribbean exam tutor, not a generic AI dashboard.

### Aesthetic Direction
Preserve the existing light editorial interface, warm cream and yellow palette, friendly illustrated brand, and strong serif/sans hierarchy. Review screens should prioritize legibility and structured explanation over decoration.

### Design Principles
- Prevent costly mistakes with calm, explicit confirmation at submission.
- Turn every review note into a specific teaching moment grounded in the learner's work.
- Make mathematical working easy to scan with clear separation between parts and lines.
- Let summaries adapt to the evidence instead of forcing every attempt into fixed generic bullets.
- Keep controls accessible and responsive without changing the established product identity.
